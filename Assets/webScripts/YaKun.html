Family Legacy

The business was carried on by Ah Koon's son and later by his grandson, Adrin Loi, who modernized and expanded the brand while keeping the traditional recipes and methods intact. Today, Ya Kun maintains the same charcoal grilling technique and authentic kaya recipe that made it famous.

Modern Era

From a single stall, Ya Kun has grown into an international chain with outlets across Asia and beyond, becoming a symbol of Singapore's food heritage. Despite its growth, Ya Kun remains committed to preserving the authentic taste and experience that customers have loved for generations.

What makes Ya Kun special is not just the ingredients, but how they're prepared.
